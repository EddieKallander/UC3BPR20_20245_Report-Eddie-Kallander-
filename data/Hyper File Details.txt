The hyper extension of the CSV files are Tableaus way of creating a native in-memory database. Providing faster query and data extraction speed.

These extracts requerie a Tableau 10.5 or newer, and are not backward compatible.

More details regarding this technology is describe on Tableau Software Websites:
https://www.tableau.com/support/hyper-resources  https://www.tableau.com/products/new-features/hyper  https://www.tableau.com/products/technology
